About Levant Strategic Centre

Our Mission

The Levant Strategic Centre is a leading research institution dedicated to providing strategic analysis and policy recommendations on the most pressing challenges facing the Middle East and Eastern Mediterranean regions.

Our Vision

To be the premier source of strategic intelligence and analysis, fostering informed decision-making and promoting stability, prosperity, and cooperation in the region.

Our Approach

We combine rigorous academic research with practical policy experience, delivering actionable insights to governments, institutions, and stakeholders across the region and beyond.

Core Values
  • Independent and objective analysis
  • Rigorous research methodology
  • Policy-relevant insights
  • Regional expertise and networks
  • Collaborative partnerships
  • Innovative thinking
